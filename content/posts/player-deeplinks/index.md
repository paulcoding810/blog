## Deeplinks for Media Players

Deeplinks allow you to open media files or streams directly in specific media players from URLs or commands. Below is a list of common deeplinks for popular players. Replace `https://` with your actual media URL.

- **IINA**: `iina://weblink?url=https%3A%2F%2F`
- **PotPlayer**: `potplayer://https//`
- **VLC**: `vlc://https//`
- **nPlayer**: `nplayer-https://`
- **OmniPlayer**: `omniplayer://weblink?url=https://`
- **Fig Player**: `figplayer://weblink?url=https://`
- **Infuse**: `infuse://x-callback-url/play?url=https://`
- **Filebox**: `filebox://play?url=https://`
- **iPlay**: `iplay://play/any?type=url&url=aHR0cHM6Ly8=`
- **MPV**: `mpv://https%3A%2F%2F`
